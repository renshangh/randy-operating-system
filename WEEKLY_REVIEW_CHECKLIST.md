# Weekly Review Checklist & Cadence

## Monday: Strategy & Alignment (20 min)
- [ ] **Reprioritize Backlog:** Review `Inbox` and `Ready` items in the GitHub Project.
- [ ] **Set Weekly Goal:** Define the single most important outcome for this week.
- [ ] **Review "Action/ThisWeek" Label:** Ensure Gmail is aligned with Project priorities.
- [ ] **Calendar Lock:** Confirm "Deep Work" blocks are clear of conflicts.

## Wednesday: Midweek Correction (10 min)
- [ ] **Velocity Check:** Are P0/P1 items moving?
- [ ] **Unblock Session:** Address any items marked `Blocked` in the last 48h.
- [ ] **Inbox Zero (Triage):** Clear `Action/Today` and `Action/ThisWeek` labels.

## Friday: Closeout & Next Week Prep (20 min)
- [ ] **Celebrate Wins:** Mark completed items as `Done` and archive finished Issues.
- [ ] **Clean Master DB:** Sync `jobs_master.csv` and ensure SQL state is healthy.
- [ ] **Refine "Next Action":** Add clear single-steps to any new items for Monday.
- [ ] **Schedule Focus Blocks:** Prep the calendar for next week's deep work.
